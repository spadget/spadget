Spadget Publishing

At Spadget Publishing, we are dedicated to publishing insightful and impactful non-fiction works that enlighten, educate, and inspire readers. As a newly established publisher, we are thrilled to introduce our debut title, The Essential Canterbury Travel Guide, which embodies our commitment to excellence in non-fiction publishing.
Our mission is to discover and amplify voices that offer unique perspectives, in-depth research, and compelling narratives on a wide range of topics. With The Essential Canterbury Travel Guide as our inaugural publication, we set the standard for the quality and integrity that will define our future catalogue.
Though we are starting with just one book, our ambition is to grow our collection with diverse and thought-provoking non-fiction works. We are actively seeking manuscripts that delve into history, science, self-improvement, memoirs, and more, aiming to provide readers with books that not only inform but also inspire action and reflection.
Whether you are an aspiring non-fiction author or a reader hungry for knowledge, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Thank you for visiting Spadget Publishing, and we look forward to sharing many more enlightening and transformative books with you in the near future.
