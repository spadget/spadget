Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and how we handle your data when you interact with our LinkedIn ads. If you have any further questions or need additional details, please let us know.

1. Introduction and Overview

Welcome to Spadget Ltd (trading as Spadget Publishing). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is not shared with third parties. This policy explains how we handle your information when you interact with our ads on LinkedIn.

2. Information Collection

We collect names and contact information directly from LinkedIn. This data helps us reach out to you with relevant promotions.

3. Use of Information

The information we gather is used for targeted promotions. We do not share your data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain collected information for as long as there are planned or ongoing book campaigns, in compliance with GDPR, which states data should be kept only as long as needed.

5. Data Security

Your information is securely stored and access is restricted to employees of Spadget Ltd. We review and update our security measures at least annually to ensure your data is protected.

6. User Controls

You have the right to control your data. You can request the deletion of your information at any time by sending a LinkedIn message.

7. Third-Party Links

Our LinkedIn ads may lead to third-party websites or services. Please review the privacy policies of these third parties directly to understand how your information will be handled there.

8. Legal Compliance

We comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations to protect your privacy. If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us via LinkedIn message.
